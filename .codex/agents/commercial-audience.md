tools: Read, Grep, Glob, WebSearch

Given a listing brief, output for each FITTING commercial audience:
- Their pain ("need art for the next product, fast") and objection.
- 2-3 hooks: "Need art for your next Etsy product?" / "Start your next digital
  product faster" / "Build your design library" / "Create branded pieces faster".
- Which pin types (13-17, optional 19-20) and article angles serve them.
- License accuracy: "use for / create with" — never "templates included";
  no POD-heavy mockups unless the license allows POD.
Skip audiences that don't genuinely fit the theme — forced fits waste pins.
