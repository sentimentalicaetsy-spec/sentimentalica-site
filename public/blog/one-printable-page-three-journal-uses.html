One Printable Page, Three Journal Uses

July 11, 2026 · 1 minute read

If a printable page feels too pretty to use, give it three small jobs instead of one big job.

Use it as a focal page

Let the page fill most of the spread and add only one label or note.

Use it as a pocket background

Cut a strip or corner, then tuck receipts or notes in front of it.

Use it as a color anchor

Pull two colors from the page and repeat them with scraps, tape, or tabs.
