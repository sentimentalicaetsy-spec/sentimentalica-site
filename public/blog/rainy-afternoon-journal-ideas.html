Rainy Afternoon Journal Ideas

July 12, 2026 · 1 minute read

A rainy afternoon is good for low-pressure journaling. The light is softer, the table feels quieter, and a page can be small without feeling unfinished.

Make One Small Page

Do not try to finish a whole section. Choose one page, one drink, one piece of paper, and one sentence.

Use the Weather as a Color

Grey-blue light works beautifully with cream paper, soft ribbon, ink brown, and one blush flower. Let the page feel like the day.

Keep It Slow

Rainy pages do not need a big idea. Tea, a letter, a flower, a window, a playlist, and one page are enough.
