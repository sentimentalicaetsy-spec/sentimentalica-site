# Sentimentalica visual brand — GROUND TRUTH (read before making any image)

Derived from `refs/branding/` by looking at the files (2026-07-09). This is the
global north star for EVERY Sentimentalica image. My earlier kraft-paper /
dusty-rose / sepia 50-things infographic was OFF-BRAND — do not reproduce it.

## Core identity
- **Base:** warm cream / ivory paper (never white, never grey).
- **Brand blue = `#163087`** (the dove blue). This is THE colour. Graphics are
  near-monochrome blue-on-cream: deep cobalt `#163087` + cornflower/lighter blue
  accents, with only small warm touches (brass, kraft, occasional soft pink/lavender).
- **The dove logo** (`refs/branding/logo.png`) recurs in almost every piece —
  usually a top corner — as a flat stamp OR a soft watercolour bird. Subtle brand
  signal, not slapped on. Composite the real logo.png in brand blue where it fits.
- **Type:** a big bold display title (bubbly or condensed) in blue, PLUS a
  handwritten script for banners, sub-lines and CTAs.
- **Motifs:** torn-paper note cards, blue gingham & polka-dot washi tape, hearts,
  four-point sparkles, botanicals (lavender, forget-me-nots, daisies).
- **Feeling:** cheerful, feminine, cottage, clean-but-warm, mobile-readable.
  Always end with a soft CTA ("link in bio", "check out my shop").

## Per-bucket look (which refs govern which image type)
- **Branded infographics** (`refs/branding/` + structure from `refs/infographics/`):
  ILLUSTRATED blue-on-cream graphics with baked-in readable text, card grids,
  cute tinted icons, the dove, a CTA. Illustrated girls are fine here.
  Text is composited by CODE (brand fonts + `#163087`), not generated by SD.
- **iPhone Notes infographic** (recurring special subtype): must look like an
  AUTHENTIC iPhone Notes screenshot — casual list, emoji, checkmarks, short
  lines. The ONE mode that deliberately is NOT the branded paper style. Natural,
  not overdesigned. (No ref file yet — Ksenia to add one, or we mock it faithfully.)
- **Realistic mockups** (`refs/scrapbook and junk jornal scenes/`): NATURAL
  photos, "not eggy" / not over-produced — a hand with rings resting on a
  sticker journal on a cafe table; an artist's desk with an open watercolour
  journal, palette and red scissors. HANDS, cropped girl, scissors, tools are
  WANTED here. Model: **Juggernaut (realistic SDXL)**. No full face needed.
- **Atmospheric scenes** (`refs/scenes/`): dreamy sunlit corners a person wants
  to escape into — plants, string lights, open window, golden light, wicker
  chair. Photoreal, warm, mood. Model: realistic.

## Human presence rule (CHANGED 2026-07-09)
The old blanket ban on hands/fingers/scissors/people is REMOVED. Hands, partial
arms, a cropped girl holding a journal, scissors and craft tools are ALLOWED and
often wanted — when natural, realistic, and reference-backed by the folders above.
Still a QA risk: deformed hands, extra fingers, creepy skin, or hands as the
focal point = REGENERATE. Product pages in a mockup must be REAL listing pages,
not AI-invented.

## The only goal that matters
Get noticed → pinned → saved → hearted → clicked through to sentimentalica.com.
Natural and saveable beats glossy and ignored.
